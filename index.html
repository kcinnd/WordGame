Game Instructions

Welcome to CyberWord! Your mission is to uncover the hidden 5-letter word within 7 attempts. Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word. Press the letters below to form your guess. After each guess, the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word. A blue glow indicates a correct letter in the incorrect position, while a green glow indicates a correct letter in the correct position. If you do not find your word by the end of the seventh round, the game starts over with a new word. Good luck, and have fun!
